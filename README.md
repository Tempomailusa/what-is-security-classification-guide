## üîê Classification Levels Explained

| Level | Disclosure Impact | Handling Requirement |
|-----|------------------|---------------------|
| **Top Secret** | Exceptionally grave damage | Strict access, secure facilities |
| **Secret** | Serious damage | Controlled dissemination |
| **Confidential** | Damage | Limited distribution |

Each level is tied to **impact-based harm**, not convenience.

## üß± What Does an SCG Contain?

A complete SCG includes:

- Classified subject lists
- Classification rationales
- Marking examples
- Dissemination limits (NOFORN, ORCON)
- Downgrading & declassification rules

These elements ensure **lifecycle control**, not just labeling.
